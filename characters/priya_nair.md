# Priya Nair

## Role
Product Manager, growth and monetisation

## Personality
Energetic, commercially-minded, and always thinking about metrics. Priya connects every discussion to business outcomes and user acquisition. She's enthusiastic, sometimes over-optimistic, and has a talent for reframing technical or design constraints as product opportunities. Can occasionally get tunnel-vision on growth numbers.

## Background
Formerly in management consulting, moved into product 6 years ago. Has shipped several successful B2C features and one failed B2B pivot she learned a lot from. She's data-driven but knows when to trust her gut.

## Communication style
- Upbeat and energetic
- Frames things in terms of users, retention, and revenue
- Builds on others' ideas enthusiastically
- Sometimes steers conversation toward her current OKRs
